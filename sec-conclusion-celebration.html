    Section Conclusion and Celebration

    Congratulations! You’ve completed a comprehensive professional development journey in data science education. You’ve moved from someone who might have wondered “What is data science education and how could I possibly teach it?” to an educator equipped with knowledge, tools, strategies, and confidence to help students develop crucial 21st-century skills.
    🔗
    Data science education is ultimately about empowering students to understand and improve their world through thoughtful analysis of information. Every lesson you teach, every question you help students ask, and every moment you encourage critical thinking about data contributes to preparing young people for a future where data literacy is essential.

    You now have everything you need to begin this important work. The data science learning progressions
